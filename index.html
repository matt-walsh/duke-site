About the Band

Hervo - Percusion

Hervo Brinado started playing precusion in his late teens, when his parents was murdered by criminals working for a local crime lord. Since then, Hervo has practiced hitting stuff with sticks, making him a natural Percussionist that The Dukes had to scoop up.

Farpaw - Bass and Vocals

By far the most charismatic of The Dukes, Farpaw grew up in a wealthy family on the South Shore of Nova Scotia with his brother Knave. The two always had a thirst for adventure. So, when they were finally ready, the two of them set off to the Cabot Trail to find it...

Knave - Guitar and Vocals

...And find it the did. While traveling, they met a kind lady by the name of Tel'Aria. They quickly bacame friends and decided to travel the trail together. Eventually, the three of them ran out of money, so they did the only logical thing: Start a band called "The Dukes of Ducarnu"

Tel'Aria - Lead Vocals

Tel'Aria grew up in the Northern tip of Cape Breaton, NS. She would spend her days wondering around the forests near her home. As she grew, so did her love of nature. After finishing college, she decided to walk the Cabot Trail, where she met Fawpaw and Knave.

Duke - Mascot

The reason for the band's finacial troubles. This lovable monster eats over $200 cdn in fresh meat every week. He's just a baby, so what is the band to do? The Dukes met this guy while wondering around the Cabot Trail, where he was picking a fight with a bear. The band saved them, and they have been together ever since.
